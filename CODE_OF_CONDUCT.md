# Code of Conduct

## Standards

Participants are expected to keep discussions professional, specific, and useful. Focus on the quality of the skill, the clarity of the instructions, and the safety of the agent behavior.

Examples of constructive participation:

- proposing clearer activation rules
- improving examples
- tightening implementation safety guidance
- reporting ambiguous or risky instructions
- explaining trade-offs with evidence

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment or personal attacks
- publishing private information
- knowingly submitting unsafe guidance
- derailing technical discussion with unrelated disputes

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject pull requests, or restrict participation when behavior interferes with a safe and useful project environment.
